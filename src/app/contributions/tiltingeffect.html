Planets of the Solar System

Mercury

The smallest planet and closest to the Sun.

Venus

The hottest planet with a thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth

The only planet known to support life.

Mars

Known as the Red Planet due to its iron-rich soil.

Jupiter

The largest planet with a massive storm called the Great Red Spot.

Saturn

Famous for its stunning ring system.

Uranus

A cold and windy planet with a unique tilt.

Neptune

The windiest planet in the solar system.
